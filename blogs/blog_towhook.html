LED Tow Hitch Cover

Introduction

Have you ever looked at your (presumably) empty tow hitch and wondered how you can make it more appealing? Towing something would definitely solve that problem. But what about the 99% of the time you more-than-likely aren't towing? An LED Tow Hitch Cover can help spice up your vehicle's rear end.

Project Overview

This Project Consists of 3 Main Components:

  • LED PCB: We will need a compact board that can fit within the tow hitch housing
  • 3D Printed, Modular, Enclosure: We will need a durable tow hitch cover enclosure that can protect the PCB and easily swap designs
  • 4-pin Trailer Connector Compatibility: We will need to plug into most vehicle's towing lighting equipment

Designing the PCB

Before designing the board, it is important to define the requirements. The board must:

  1. Be powered by vehicle's 12V architecture
  2. Be bright enough to see both in the day and night
  3. Stay cool within the enclosure
